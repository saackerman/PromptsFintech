# PromptsFintech

A curated collection of prompts for testing and comparing AI capabilities across various fintech scenarios.

## Purpose

This repository provides a standardized set of prompts to evaluate and compare different AI systems (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, etc.) on fintech-related tasks. Use these prompts to:

- Assess AI understanding of financial concepts
- Compare accuracy across different AI platforms
- Evaluate compliance and risk awareness
- Test practical fintech use cases

## Categories

### 1. Fraud Detection
Prompts for testing AI's ability to identify fraudulent patterns and suspicious activities.

### 2. Risk Assessment
Scenarios requiring AI to evaluate financial risks and provide mitigation strategies.

### 3. Financial Analysis
Prompts for analyzing financial statements, market trends, and business metrics.

### 4. Compliance & Regulations
Testing knowledge of financial regulations (KYC, AML, GDPR, etc.) and compliance requirements.

### 5. Customer Service
Common customer support scenarios in banking and fintech applications.

### 6. Investment Recommendations
Prompts for evaluating investment strategies and portfolio management.

### 7. Payment Processing
Scenarios related to payment systems, transactions, and settlement.

### 8. Credit Scoring
Prompts for credit evaluation and lending decisions.

## How to Use

1. **Select a Category**: Choose the fintech domain you want to test
2. **Pick a Prompt**: Select one or more prompts from that category
3. **Test Across AIs**: Use the same prompt with different AI systems
4. **Evaluate Responses**: Compare using the evaluation criteria provided
5. **Document Results**: Record your findings for comparison

## Evaluation Criteria

When comparing AI responses, consider:

- **Accuracy**: Correctness of financial concepts and calculations
- **Compliance Awareness**: Understanding of regulations and legal requirements
- **Risk Identification**: Ability to spot potential issues
- **Clarity**: How well the response is explained
- **Practicality**: Real-world applicability of suggestions
- **Ethical Considerations**: Awareness of ethical implications
